Getting started with Hivra takes under two minutes. You sign up at [hivra.app](https://hivra.app), choose how you want to authenticate, and land on your dashboard ready to connect platforms and publish. This page covers each sign-up method, email verification, and what you get on each plan.

## Create your account

Visit [hivra.app/signup](https://hivra.app/signup) and choose one of three sign-up paths.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Continue with Google">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Continue with Google">
        On the sign-up page, click **Continue with Google**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your Google account">
        Pick the Google account you want to use for Hivra from the account picker.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Click **Continue** on the Google authorization screen. You are signed in immediately — no email verification step required.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Continue with Meta">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Continue with Meta">
        On the sign-up page, click **Continue with Meta**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authorize with Facebook">
        Click **Continue as [your account]** on the Meta login screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Grant page access">
        Click **Opt in to all current and future Pages** so Hivra can publish to your Facebook Pages later.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Finish authorization">
        Click **Continue**, then **Save**, then **Got it**. You are signed in immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Continue with Email">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Continue with Email">
        On the sign-up page, click **Continue with Email**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your details">
        Fill in your name, email address, and a password, then click **Create account**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify your email">
        Check your inbox for a confirmation email from Hivra. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder. Click the button in the email to confirm your address and activate your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## First-time onboarding

After signing in for the first time, Hivra walks you through a short setup flow. You describe how you plan to use the product and connect your first platforms. You can skip this flow and return to **Integrations** at any time to connect accounts.

## Plans

Hivra has three plans. You start on Free automatically when you create your account.

<Note>
  The Free plan never expires and requires no credit card. You can stay on Free as long as you like and upgrade when you're ready.
</Note>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free — $0/month" icon="circle">
    **5 uploads per month**, post scheduling, bulk uploads, and first-comment support. One workspace, one seat. Ideal for creators who are just getting started and want to try Hivra before committing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Creator — $15/month" icon="star">
    **Unlimited uploads**, all six platforms, comment management, and analytics. One workspace and one seat. Add-on workspaces ($10/mo each) and extra seats ($5/mo each) available. Best for solo creators publishing regularly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Card title="Business — $30/month" icon="building">
  Everything in Creator, plus **5 workspaces and 5 team seats**. Includes team collaboration features and publishing approval workflows — so teammates can submit posts for review before anything goes live. Built for agencies and teams managing multiple brands.
</Card>

<Warning>
  The Free plan caps at 5 completed publishes per calendar month (UTC). Drafts and scheduled posts that have not yet published do not count against the cap.
</Warning>

For a side-by-side feature comparison and annual pricing details, see [Plans](/account/plans).
