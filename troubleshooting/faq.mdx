These are the questions that come up most often from Hivra users. If you don't find your answer here, reach out to the team at [support@hivra.app](mailto:support@hivra.app).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What platforms does Hivra support?">
    Hivra currently supports publishing to **YouTube**, **Instagram**, **TikTok**, **Facebook**, **X**, and **Threads**. You connect each platform through **Integrations** using the platform's official authorization flow.

    Additional platforms may be added in the future based on user demand. If there is a platform you need that is not listed, let the team know at [support@hivra.app](mailto:support@hivra.app).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many videos can I publish on the Free plan?">
    The Free plan includes **5 publishes per month**. This quota resets at the start of each billing month. Once you reach the limit, new publish and schedule requests will be held until the quota resets or you upgrade your plan.

    If you need to publish more frequently, the Creator and Business plans offer unlimited uploads. You can upgrade at any time from **Billing** in your account settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I write different captions for each platform?">
    Yes. Hivra lets you customize captions, hashtags, and other settings on a per-platform basis inside the **Composer**. When you select multiple destinations for a post, a per-platform drawer appears for each one, giving you a separate field to tailor the text before you publish or schedule.

    By default, Hivra can pre-fill all platforms with the same caption so you are not starting from scratch on each one — just edit the platforms where you want something different.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why aren't my analytics showing data?">
    A few things can cause analytics to appear empty or incomplete:

    - **Data lag:** Metrics from platforms typically take a few minutes — and sometimes longer — to propagate after a post goes live. Wait a short while and use the manual refresh option if available.
    - **Platform availability:** Not all platforms expose the same metrics through their APIs. If a specific metric is missing for a particular platform, it may not be available through Hivra's integration for that network.
    - **Connection health:** Analytics syncing requires a healthy connection. If the account shows **Attention** in **Integration Health,** reconnect it so data can resume syncing.
    - **Plan access:** Analytics features are limited to paid plans. Check your current plan in **Billing** if a section appears locked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I publish images and text posts, not just videos?">
    Yes. Although Hivra is optimized for short-form vertical video, you can also publish image and text posts to supported platforms. The **Composer** handles these content types when they are supported by the destination platform's API.

    Keep in mind that some platforms have specific restrictions on text-only or image posts, so results may vary depending on your destinations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my published posts if I disconnect an account?">
    Disconnecting an account in **Integrations** removes Hivra's access to that platform, but it does not affect content already published. Posts that went live before you disconnected remain on the platform exactly as they were — Hivra does not delete them.

    After disconnecting, Hivra can no longer publish new content to that account or sync new analytics and comments from it. Any scheduled posts that target the disconnected account will fail at their scheduled time. Reconnect the account when you are ready to resume publishing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the approval workflow work?">
    Publishing approvals are available on the **Creator** and **Business** plans. When a workspace Member submits a post for publishing or scheduling, it enters an **Awaiting approval** state instead of going live immediately. The workspace Owner or an Admin with approval permissions can then review the post in the **Library** under the **Approvals** filter.

    From there, the reviewer can approve the post (allowing it to publish or proceed to its scheduled time) or reject it (sending it back to the Member). Owners always have full publishing freedom within their own workspace and are never subject to approval gates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel or change my subscription?">
    Go to **Plan & billing** in your account settings to manage your subscription. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there. When you cancel, your current plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle — you will not lose access immediately. At the end of the cycle, your workspace moves to the Free plan.

    If you have questions about billing or a charge, contact [support@hivra.app](mailto:support@hivra.app).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact Hivra support?">
    You can reach the Hivra support team by email at [support@hivra.app](mailto:support@hivra.app). Include as much detail as you can — the platform affected, what you were trying to do, and any error messages you saw — so the team can help you faster.
  </Accordion>
