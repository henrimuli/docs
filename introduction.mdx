Hivra is the video publishing engine built for content creators and teams. Instead of logging into six different apps to post the same clip, you upload once in Hivra and push to every platform you're connected to — with per-platform captions, thumbnails, and scheduling controls all in one workspace.

## What Hivra does

Hivra centralizes the entire short-form video workflow: upload, caption, schedule, monitor comments, and measure performance — without bouncing between apps.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [hivra.app](https://hivra.app) — the Free plan requires no credit card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your platforms">
    Go to **Integrations** and link your social accounts through each platform's official authorization flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and compose">
    Click **New Post**, drop your video, write captions, and pick your target accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish or schedule">
    Hit **Publish now** to go live immediately, or pick a date and time to schedule for later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
